Delete Your Account

Knowear App by Knoverse

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Knowear account and all associated data, you can do so by following the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to support@knoverse.io with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your Knowear account.

Request Account Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information - your name, email address, and profile data
  • Wardrobe items - all clothing photos, descriptions, and metadata
  • Outfits - all outfit combinations you have created
  • Calendar events - all outfit plans and internal events
  • Preferences - all app settings and personalization data
  • Usage history - wear tracking and activity logs

Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days of your request. You will not be able to recover any wardrobe items, outfits, or other data after deletion.

Data Retention

After receiving your deletion request, we will process it within 30 days. During this period, your account will be deactivated. Some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention), but will be deleted as soon as the retention period ends.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at support@knoverse.io.
